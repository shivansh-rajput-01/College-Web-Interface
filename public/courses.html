B.Tech – Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

The B.Tech program in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) provides students with a solid foundation in computer systems, programming, software development, and algorithm design. Students will engage in hands-on learning in various areas, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, and software engineering. The course prepares students for a wide range of careers in tech industries.

  • Duration: 8 Semesters (4 Years)
  • Qualification Required: 12th (PCM) or equivalent
  • Fees per Semester: ₹80,000
  • Total Fees: ₹6,40,000

B.Tech – Robotics & AI

The B.Tech program in Robotics & Artificial Intelligence is designed to equip students with advanced knowledge and skills in robotics, machine learning, and AI technologies. Students will gain hands-on experience in designing intelligent systems, automation, and smart robots, making them industry-ready for the rapidly growing fields of robotics and AI.

  • Duration: 8 Semesters (4 Years)
  • Qualification Required: 12th (PCM) with IP/IT/CS as an optional subject
  • Fees per Semester: ₹1,00,000
  • Total Fees: ₹8,00,000

M.Tech – ML

The M.Tech program in Machine Learning (ML) focuses on building a strong foundation in algorithms, data structures, and deep learning. This course prepares students for advanced roles in AI, data science, and ML, with a deep understanding of model development, data analysis, and real-world applications of machine learning techniques.

  • Duration: 4 Semesters (2 Years)
  • Qualification Required: BS in CS or related fields (AI, ML, DS)
  • Fees per Semester: ₹55,000
  • Total Fees: ₹2,20,000

M.S. – Data Science

The M.S. in Data Science program is designed to train students in data mining, statistical analysis, and the use of data analytics tools. Students will explore big data technologies and the application of data science techniques in various industries like healthcare, finance, and more. This course aims to develop expertise in data-driven decision-making processes.

  • Duration: 4 Semesters (2 Years)
  • Qualification Required: BS in CS or related fields (AI, ML, DS)
  • Fees per Semester: ₹55,000
  • Total Fees: ₹2,20,000

BS – IoT

The Bachelor of Science (BS) in IoT focuses on the development of connected devices and smart networks. This course covers sensor technologies, networking, cloud computing, and embedded systems to build the foundation for designing scalable IoT systems. Students will learn to create innovative solutions for the interconnected world.

  • Duration: 8 Semesters (4 Years)
  • Qualification Required: 12th (PCM)
  • Fees per Semester: ₹50,000
  • Total Fees: ₹4,00,000

Ph.D – Robotics

The Ph.D. in Robotics offers in-depth research opportunities in robotics, automation, and intelligent systems. Scholars will engage in cutting-edge research to contribute to the development of autonomous robots, AI-powered systems, and advanced robotics technologies. This program emphasizes both theoretical understanding and practical applications in the ever-evolving robotics domain.

  • Duration: 10 Semesters (5 Years)
  • Qualification Required: MS/M.Tech in Robotics, AI, ML, or IoT
  • Fees per Semester: ₹10,000
  • Total Fees: ₹1,00,000
