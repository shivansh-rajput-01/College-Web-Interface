Institute Of Robotics Intelligence

About the Institute

The Institute of Robotics & Intelligence is a premier educational hub committed to fostering innovation and excellence in next-gen technologies. With a vision to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications, the institute provides a holistic learning environment. Our interdisciplinary approach in engineering and research ensures that students are industry-ready and innovation-driven. From modern classrooms to world-class labs, we create an ecosystem that empowers students to lead in the fields of robotics, artificial intelligence, and emerging technologies. The institute is home to brilliant minds guided by expert faculty and mentors who bring in both academic and industry insights.

Courses Offered

The institute offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs tailored to meet the demands of modern technology. Courses include B.Tech, M.Tech, MS, BS, and Ph.D. in specialized domains such as Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Electronics & Communication (EC), Robotics & AI, Machine Learning (ML), and Internet of Things (IoT). These programs are structured with a blend of core theoretical knowledge, practical lab exposure, project work, and industry internships to make students technically sound and globally competent.

Advanced Research & Innovation

Research is at the heart of the institute’s mission. We host multiple research centers including a Centre for AI & ML, IoT Innovation Lab, and a Robotics Simulation Lab. Students and scholars work on real-world projects in collaboration with industry leaders, tackling challenges across automation, intelligent systems, smart devices, and data science. With cutting-edge infrastructure and dedicated funding support, our research output has contributed to patents, publications, and product innovations.

Campus & Learning Environment

The lush green and digitally equipped campus provides a perfect balance of nature and technology. Smart classrooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, modern hostels, recreational spaces, and 24x7 accessible labs make learning seamless and enjoyable. The institute encourages self-paced and mentor-guided learning approaches to promote deeper understanding and creativity among students. Various student clubs, innovation cells, and cultural events add vibrancy to campus life.

Placements & Industry Connect

The institute maintains strong ties with top companies and research organizations in the tech domain. Regular workshops, guest lectures, and industrial visits ensure that students stay in sync with current industry trends. Our dedicated placement cell works closely with startups and global tech giants, offering students internship and placement opportunities in companies like Google, TCS, DRDO, Nvidia, Intel, and various robotics-based startups.

Student Life & Activities

Beyond academics, the institute offers a dynamic student life filled with innovation challenges, hackathons, robotics expos, coding marathons, and leadership programs. Student clubs in AI, electronics, coding, and robotics foster creativity and teamwork. The institute encourages every student to take ownership of their learning journey, shaping not just engineers, but future leaders.

Global Collaborations & Future Vision

With MOUs signed with leading universities and research bodies across the globe, our students get access to exchange programs, joint research, and international internships. The institute’s long-term vision is to be a global center of excellence in intelligent technologies and advanced robotics, nurturing innovators who will shape the future.
